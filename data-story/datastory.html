While certain games are more successful than others in terms of completion rate, it is unlikely that developers will move to only making short, challenging, moderately priced Puzzle games. However, this story shows that larger games need to respect their long runtime and plan on how to keep the player entertained, players will definitely not stick to a game just because they paid a lot of money for it, and free games may not seem as successful as they may often only be placed in a Steam library and not started.
